Assignments

Students will be evaluated on their knowledge of course content and their ability to communicate and apply their understanding via a practical portfolio (50%) and an individual project (50%).

Both assignments should be handed in electronically on or before 19 February 2023. Please contact the course instructor if you want an extension for any reason.

Practical portfolio (50%)

Work produced in each practical session will be compiled into a portfolio assignment to be submitted at the end of the course. The practical portfolio will evaluate students’ knowledge and understanding of the course content.

Individual project (50%)

The individual project will assess students’ ability to communicate and apply the skills acquired during the course by producing a publication-quality map with archaeological data.
